About Me

My name is Cedrine Gwen Bautista, 20 years old, and I live in Balanga, Bataan. I take pride in my strong connection with my community, which has influenced my values and shaped my goals. Growing up here has taught me the importance of hard work, collaboration, and giving back.

Sports are a big part of my life, and I'm passionate about staying active. I enjoy playing basketball, volleyball, badminton, table tennis, and billiards. Engaging in these activities not only helps me stay physically fit but also teaches me important lessons. Through sports, I learn about teamwork, perseverance, and sportsmanship. It's rewarding to push myself and work together with others towards a common goal.

Besides sports, I also enjoy entertaining myself with various activities. I find joy in watching Korean dramas, keeping up with the latest sports anime, and reading webtoons. I also love driving around. These hobbies allow me to immerse myself in captivating stories and characters. They serve as a much-needed escape from the stresses of daily life. Engaging in these activities helps me relax and unwind after a long day.

Academically, I am committed to my studies and aim for excellence in all my endeavors. Currently, I am the class mayor of BS Information Technology NW2A and serve as a committee member of Membership and Election Committee in SSITE (Student Society on Information Technology Education). These roles provide me with opportunities to actively contribute to the school community and enhance my leadership skills. I take pride in being able to support and represent my classmates effectively. Through these responsibilities, I strive to make a positive impact and inspire others to excel academically.

When it comes to my personality, I consider myself to be thoughtful, courageous, humorous, open-minded, friendly, and considerate. I believe in treating others with kindness and respect, and I'm always ready to help those in need. Forming meaningful connections with people is a priority for me, and I value the friendships and relationships I've cultivated over time. Being able to positively impact others and create lasting bonds brings me a sense of fulfillment and happiness. Overall, I strive to embody these traits in my interactions with others and in my everyday life.
